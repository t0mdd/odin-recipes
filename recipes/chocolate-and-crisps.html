Chocolate and crisps

Description

An adventurous combination. Dazzle your guests with this controversial nibble.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (Preferably Maris Piper)
  • Salt
  • Oil (Olive, peanut or canola)
  • Chocolate (Any type)

Steps

  1. Slice those taters thin
  2. Cover with lashings of oil and salt, place on a baking tray and roast for 1 hour on 500 degrees Celsius
  3. Chop your chocolate into a fine dust and sprinkle onto the crisps immediately after removing them from the oven
  4. Consume within 10 minutes to avoid hardening the chocolate!
