Pork Loaf

Description

A true British classic. Squidgy breads that combine with tough porcine meats to create a taste so fresh you'd think you were eating a live pig in a freshly baked bread casing!

Ingredients

  • PORK Granules
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Bread sauce
  • the jelly from pork pies

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into your favourite mixing bowl
  2. Mix together with a whisk
  3. Roast in the oven for 8 hours on 300 degrees Celsius
  4. Remove from the oven and consume immediately
